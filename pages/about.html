Target Audiences

（TBD）The public audience that would visit DIA who is keen to learn about art, history, and critical theories. This could also be community building through educational workshops and collaborative development creation with cultural institutions and community organizations.

Data, Information and Content

（TBD）The core data for this project originates from the exquisite Asian art pieces from the DIA, and we will utilize the art pieces to create 3D models, which allows for an immersive and interactive experience for users. In addition, we are also adding on the historical descriptions, pictures, links, and references from other museums, digital libraries, or archives that are connected with the current collection piece.
